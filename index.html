Feed a Friend – Helping Street Dogs & Cows

Why This Mission Matters

Every day, hundreds of street dogs and cows struggle to find safe food and water. A small act of kindness from us makes a big difference in their lives.

How You Can Help

  • Give clean water and simple food like roti, rice, biscuits
  • Never feed spicy or oily food
  • Keep a small bowl of water outside your home
  • Call local NGOs if an injured animal needs help
  • Feed cows with vegetables, banana, grass, or roti
